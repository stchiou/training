On Boarding

On Board Notification

The notification of onboarding will be received from Department Administration, every Friday through email. Current Department Administrative Representative is Wendy Richardson. The notification should include User ID, employee's name, supervisor's name, manager's name, employee's role in the Department, as well as the date that the employee will attend New Employee Orientation.

Reach out to the Department Administrative Representative if email notification for the following week is not received before Friday noon.

Once the notification is received, the initial training request should be scheduled. In general, a new employee (including colleagues and contractor) will attend the Site New Employee Orientation starting from the Monday and being released back to the CQ Lab on Wednesday afternoon on the following week after the notification is received.

The initial training for onbarding employee is to be scheduled through both PLS Request Form and regular meeting scheduling process. The initial training includes both core training, which is a departmental requirements, and role-specific training.

Subsequent Training

Assignment Profile and curricula can be added and removed from colleague's PLS account any time after Initial Training has been scheduled.

Add Curricula and Remove

More assignment profiles or curricula can be added into colleague's PLS through additional PLS Request Form. A supervisor's signature is required in order to make modification. This process, however, does not allow any individual item, such as COURSE, SOP, OJT etc. to be added/remove without adding or removing of the parent curriculum or assignment profile of the item to be added or removed.

The information needed for the modification process is:
  • ID and name of the colleague
  • Code of Assignment Profiles or curricula to be added or removed
  • Justification for modification of the training
  • Supervisor's name and signature

Extension Request

Once the learning curricula has been assigned to a colleague, the items included in the curricula have due dates associated with them. In general, the default due date is 90 days from the date when the curriculum is assigned. Colleague will need to complete the assignment within the 90 days period. Failing to complete the assigned item before due date will result in the revoking of the gate access priviledge of the badge.

The Site Training allows due date of certain assigned items to be extended with prior approval from the Training Department. The extension request can be made with the Extension Request Form. The first extension can be requested at the maximal of 90 days. The subsequent extension request, in general, will be granted for 30 days with some exceptions. The justification of the extension needs to be provided.

The due date of the items that can be extended are:
  • Student Guide, SG
  • Structured On the Job Training, SOJT
  • Task Qualification, TQ
  • Site-wide instructor-led couses, due to availability

Certified Fellow Employee

The purpose of identifying and establishing a Certified Fellow Employee is to serves as a coach/trainer or go-to person to facilitate the training process in the CQ Lab. The following criteria are to be met to become eligible as a CFE:

  • Be a permanent employee of the company
  • Have attened the Certified Fellow Employee Training
  • Be a certified incumben and proficient in the the job on whiche the trainee is to be trained
  • Must be selected and approved by supervisor based on performance based criteria
  • Be up-to-date on their training qualifications prior to training another trainee.

Typically, the process is initiated by a Supervisor filling out the Certified Trainer Selection Form. The form requires signatures from both Supervisor and Department Manager prior to submission for final approval by the Site Training Department. The designee of the form will then require to participate and get credit from a session of instructor-led training class, Certified Fellow Employee Training course PGM-RMT-OETR_CFE. This training class is offered by the Site Training throughout the year. The available schedue are:

This form may also be used as a Training Exemption Form for those individuals who will be considered a Qalified Instructor and will be exempt from the course based on previous experience and/or as they are the first person performing the training.
