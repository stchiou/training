On Boarding

On Board Notification

The notification of onboarding will be received from Department Administration, every Friday through email. Current Department Administrative Representative is Wendy Richardson. The notification should include User ID, employee's name, supervisor's name, manager's name, employee's role in the Department, as well as the date that the employee will attend New Employee Orientation.

Reach out to the Department Administrative Representative if email notification for the following week is not received before Friday noon.

Once the notification is received, the initial training request should be scheduled. In general, a new employee (including colleagues and contractor) will attend the Site New Employee Orientation starting from the Monday and being released back to the CQ Lab on Wednesday afternoon on the following week after the notification is received.

The initial training for onbarding employee is to be scheduled through both PLS Request Form and regular meeting scheduling process. The initial training includes core training, which is a departmental requirements, and role- specific training.

Compliance Specialist, PGM-RMT-QACQ_COMPL_SPEC

This assignment profile provides required training for accessing ExcelSafe (Compliant version of Excel Spreadsheet that keeps records of control substances). The following items are required for accessing ExcelSafe :

  1. ExcelSafe Access Form (USRM-FRM-00603, aka FORM-65483). The following are required information:
    • User Name field is the name of the requester
    • UID field is Hospira ID (GUID for employee after 2018)
    • Access Activity: check Add box
    • Access Level: check Data Entry
  2. Proof of completion of training:

    Provide a copy of Certificate of Completion printed out from PLS

The form and the certificate should then submitted to Theresa Khelifi in Trailer I after obtaining Supervisor's signature.
